# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Sales agents and prospective homebuyers at the Ny'ah Phú Định Sale Gallery. The agents speak, and homebuyers look at a large TV/tablet screen displaying dynamic slides.

## Product Purpose
An ambient real-time slideshow dashboard that listens to gallery conversations and automatically renders beautiful, highly readable slides (floor plans, images, features, maps) matching the conversation topics instantly.

## Brand Personality
Sophisticated, trustworthy, premium boutique real estate. Clean typography, high contrast, elegant dark and light variants.

## Anti-references
SaaS-cliché metrics templates, over-rounded card grids, tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows on every section, and low-contrast grey text.

## Design Principles
1. **Gallery Legibility**: Slide elements and text must be readable from a distance (3-5 meters from a TV screen).
2. **Contextual Synergy**: Images and maps must dynamically match the spoken context (bếp, vị trí, phòng ngủ).
3. **Restrained Elegance**: Use high-quality photography, structured layouts (split, full background), and clean spacing instead of decorative dividers or complex widgets.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High contrast (minimum 4.5:1), large default text, support for reduced motion, and instant transitions for quick glanceability.
